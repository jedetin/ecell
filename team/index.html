Directorate

Directorate of E-cell

Motivated by the vision and mission of the University, the students of ECell are encouraged to pursue their entrepreneurial ventures and are motivated to understand the real meaning of ‘Entrepreneurship’ by the Directorate of E-cell. The function of the members of the directorate is to nourish and nurture ideas and give support to the students in becoming an impactful entrepreneur.
The Directorate of E-Cell raises the curtain from the creative and innovative minds under the active supervision of :
